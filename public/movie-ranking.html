Best Animes based on myself.

Animes listed are not according to rankings.

1. Reincarnate as a Slime.

Genre: Isekai

A man died in the main world and got reincarnated in another world as a slime where he became an OP (Overpowered)
individual. From there he manage to be a sworn friend to a dragon who is called Veldora.

2. SAO - Sword Art Online

Genre: Game

An online game called MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) has became dangerous when the
players could not logged out from the game. Those who died in the game, officially dies in the real world. Thus this created
confusion and fear among players as they do not want to die in the real world thus playing the game safely.

3. Overlord IV

Genre: Game, Isekai

A game where it is too old that people leave the game one by one until the last player is left, of course
it is the main character. He watched the game countdown to the end and he closed his eyes thinking that
it is the end for him. Little did he knows that he is now inside the main game where he can communicate
with all the NPC (Non-Player Characters).
